Rules of the Game

  1. The goal is to have your cards add up to a sum greater than the Dealer's.
  2. You can request another card by clicking the 'Hit' button.
  3. When the you are satisfied with the sum of your cards, hit the 'Stay' button and the Dealer's turn begins.
  4. If the sum of your cards (or the Dealer's) is greater than 21, then you 'Bust' and lose automatically.
  5. Number cards have a value equal to their face value (eg. 4 of hearts has a value of 4).
  6. Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) have the value of 10.
  7. Ace cards have alternating values, 1 or 11, depending on whether the value is adventageous to a win.
